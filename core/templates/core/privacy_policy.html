Privacy Policy

Welcome to Thriftly, your go-to marketplace for buying and selling second-hand treasures. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Transaction Data: Information about the items you buy, sell, or save on our platform.
  • Usage Data: We collect information such as IP addresses, device details, browser type, and browsing behavior for analytics and security purposes.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to improve your experience and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our marketplace services.
  • Facilitate transactions and secure payments.
  • Enhance user experience and recommend relevant products.
  • Prevent fraud, enforce our policies, and ensure platform security.
  • Send updates, promotions, or important notifications (you can opt out at any time).

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure payment gateways, and regular security audits. However, no online platform is 100% secure, so we encourage users to take precautions, such as using strong passwords and avoiding sharing sensitive details.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (for payments, hosting, and analytics).
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our users.
  • Other users, when necessary (e.g., sharing your contact details with a buyer/seller after a confirmed transaction).

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account information.
  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link.
  • Disable cookies through browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

Thriftly may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, so we recommend reviewing their policies before sharing personal information.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
